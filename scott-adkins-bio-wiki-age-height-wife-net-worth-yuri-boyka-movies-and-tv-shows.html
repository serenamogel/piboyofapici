Who is Scott Adkins? Biography and Wiki

Scott Adkins born Scott Edward Adkins is an English actor and martial artist best known for portraying Yuri Boyka in the Undisputed franchise, and as Casey Bowman in the 2009 film Ninja and its 2013 sequel Ninja: Shadow of a Tear. He has also had a supporting role in Doctor Strange and The Expendables 2.

Scott Adkins Career

Adkins first became interested in martial arts at the age of ten, when he visited a local Judo club with his father and older brother. At age 13, he was robbed and his interest in martial arts grew even more. That is when he started to practice Taekwondo, receiving his black belt at the age of 19.

Scott Adkins martial art

Adkins also started to practice Kickboxing since the age of 16 with Anthony Jones. He eventually became a kickboxing instructor for the Professional Karate Association (PKA). Scott also has experience in Ninjutsu, Karate, Wushu, Jiujitsu, Muay Thai, Capoeira, and Acrobatic Gymnastics.

His first break came when he was offered a role in a Hong Kong martial arts film called Dei Seung Chui Keung (2001) (aka Extreme Challenge). Acting roles started to come in and he was offered a guest role in BBC’s Doctors (2000). He appeared in a few episodes in BBC’s EastEnders (1985) and City Central (1998), and a lead role in Sky One comedy drama Mile High (2003) followed by a regular role in BBC’s Holby City (1999) as Bradley Hume.

Scott Adkins Yuri Boyka

Adkins portrayed Talbot in Special Forces (2003) and Yuri Boyka in Undisputed II: Last Man Standing (2006). It was this film that broke him into the mainstream with his villainous portrayal of the Russian MMA underground fighter Boyka who fight against the current Heavyweight Champ George “Iceman” Chambers(Michael Jai White) who is sent to a Russian Jail on trumped-up drug charges.

Adkins Credits

After this Scott had guest-starring roles in bigger budget films like The Bourne Ultimatum (2007) and The Tournament (2009) and played Jean-Claude Van Damme’s main adversary in Sony Pictures The Shepherd: Border Patrol (2008). Adkins appears in the role of King Amphitryon in The Legend of Hercules. In 2012, he was cast to star in Métal Hurlant Chronicles, a sci-fi Franco-Belgium TV show.

Adkins has enjoyed a long career in the entertainment industry spanning almost two and a half decades. Through proceeds from his work as an actor and screenwriter/producer, he has been able to accumulate a huge fortune. Scott is estimated to have a net worth of about $3 million.
