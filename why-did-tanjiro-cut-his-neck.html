Why did Tanjiro cut his neck?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 29, 2024
Thanks to some outside help from Nezuko, Tanjiro realizes he's in a dream. Desperate to escape, he runs around (and has a tearful farewell with his family) and tries to find the way out. When he doesn't see one, Tanjiro makes the quick and brutal decision to cut his own neck and commit suicide.

Why is Tanjiro's head so hard?

Tanjiro's head is hard to crack to the extreme! The major literary reason for Tanjiro having such a hard head is to be symbolic of his determination and fighting style. Time and time again, Tanjiro is shown to press forward and fight on even when all hope seems lost.

How is Tanjiro's smell so strong?
