Lil Kim's Daughter Poses in a Teal Shirt See the Adorable Photo Her Ex, Mr Papers Posted

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, January 30, 2024

Lil Kim's and Mr. Papers' daughter Royal Reign looked painfully cute in a new photo shared by her father on Instagram and fans say she looks just like him.

Seems like yesterday that Lil Kim and her rapper ex, Mr. Papers, welcomed their daughter, Royal Reign into the world but she is already so big.

Not only is Reign 6 years old, but she is also learning how to pose for cute Instagram pictures and seems to be doing a great job.

THE APPLE OF HIS EYE

In an Instagram post, Mr. Papers shared a snapshot of his daughter looking all cute and lively in a blue tank top with floral motifs.

Upon focusing the eyes on the typography of the top, one can see that it reads "Mom is my favorite." Whoever her favorite parent might be, it is pretty obvious that both her parents love her to bits.

Reign recently celebrated her 6th birthday with her parents with a party in the park. The fairytale party was complete with a pony and "Frozen" themed presents.

In an Instagram video that the rapper shared on July 27, he was seen kissing Kim's lips...

REIGN IS SURROUNDED BY LOVE

The little one looked like a real princess with a dreamy pink dress with a tulle skirt that came alive with pastel colors. Kim and Mr. Papers gave their fans a peep into the celebrations through Instagram videos and photos.

According to Kim, Mr. Papers did the hard work to organize the fun party for their precious daughter. They all had a great time together as a family.

Lil Kim at the MTV VMAs on August 26, 2019. | Source: Getty Images

It is unclear whether Kim and Mr. Papers are back together, but the rapper's recent Instagram posts likely suggest that they have chosen to be together again.

In a video that the rapper shared on July 27, he was seen kissing Kim's lips while he went love-crazy on her as they held each other.

HINTS OF REKINDLED ROMANCE

He shared another video on August 11 where he was wearing gold eye masks while talking about how amazing his hair looks. Although Kim was not seen in the video, she touched his hair as she complimented his look.

Kim and Mr. Papers' relationship had gotten so rocky in the past that their daughter's custody became a major issue with the rapper even releasing a freestyle rap about it.
