Manu Rishi Chadha is a film actor, lyricist, script and dialogue writer, hailing from India and work in the Hindi cinema. He has worked in several Indian movies and also appeared in web series “Mirzapur” Roommates” and others. He got trained under the supervision of theatre director Arvind Gaur and he worked along with him for six years. He has made his career broad and won the hearts of many with his amazing contribution to the Bollywood film industry.

Career

Manu Rishi Chadha has started his career working as a theatre artist and appeared in various plays in which he performed well in unique roles. Later, he moved into the Hindi movies and worked in a large number of movies. Initially, he worked as a theatre artist with the Asmita theatre group in Delhi, India. Also, he has worked in more than 40 plays alongside the famed theatre director Arvind Gaur.

Some of his most popular plays are “Rakht Kalyan” by Girish Karnad, “Final Solutions” by Mahesh Dattani, “Court Martial” by Swadesh Deepak, “Julius Caesar” by William Shakespeare, “Andha Yug” by Dharamvir Bharati and others that are worth sharing.

In 2001, he decided to move to Mumbai to pursue a career in the field of acting in movies. However, he moved into Mumbai and started appearing in the Bollywood movies. Fortunately, he got the chance to work in the movie “Saathiya” featuring Vivek Oberoi and Rani Mukherjee. In the movie, he portrayed the role of a doctor. While he worked in more than 20 movies and got much recognition.

Achievements

Among various notable achievements, Manu Rishi Chadha was awarded the IIFA Best Dialogue Award for “Oye Lucky! Lucky Oye”.
