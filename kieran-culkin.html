Kieran Culkin Age, Siblings, Wife, Children, Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, March 15, 2024

Kieran Culkin is an American actor who started his professional acting career as a child starring as Fuller McCallister in the Home Alone franchise in 1990. However, he made his breakout performance with the role of Jason Slocumb, Jr. in the film Igby Goes Down in 2002.

As of now, he's starring in the satirical drama television series, Succession on HBO. And it was premiered on June 3, 2018. Today let's talk about Culkin's personal and professional life. 

Who is Kieran Culkin Married to?

Two times Young Artist Award nominee Kieran Culkin is a married man. He has tied the knot with his long term girlfriend turned wife, Jazz Charton. She is a London-born former model.

Culkin and Charton first met each other in September 2012 at a bar in New York. Ever since they have been enjoying each other company without any conflicts. Finally, after one year of love romance, they decided to take the relationship to the next level while they were on a road trip around the US.

On June 18, 2013, the couple officially exchanged vows in Decorah, Iowa. Sharing the photo of a marriage certificate on her Instagram, Jazz wrote, " Official!". How Much is Kieran Culkin Net Worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Kieran Culkin has an impressive net worth of $5 million. He has acquired his fortune from his successful acting career. Since 1990, he has been active in the entertainment industry.

As per his salary, Culkin is said to be making more than $300,000 per episode of HBO's hit TV show, Succession. While Brian Cox who plays Logan Roy is known to be the highest-paid cast member with a salary of $400,000 to $500,000 per episode.

Along with this, he has worked in numerous series and projects on TV shows like Saturday Night Live, The Magical Legend of the Leprechauns, Fargo, Long Live the Royals and Go Fish. His portrayed films Home Alone and Home Alone 2: Lost in New York has earned $476.7 million and $359 million respectively. 

His other hit significant film includes Igby Goes Down, The Cider House Rules, She's All That, Lymelife, Hero Squad, Movie 43, Quitters, and Infinity Baby. Added to that, he has starred in stage production such as This Is Our Youth, After Ashley, and subUrbia.

Bio and Early Life

On September 30, 1982, Kieran Culkin was born in New York, New York, the United States as Kieran Kyle Culkin. His parents are Christopher Culkin, a stage actor and his wife, Patricia Brentrup, a telephone operator.

Culkin has seven siblings named Macaulay Culkin, Rory Culkin, Dakota Culkin, Shane Culkin, Quinn Culkin, Christian Culkin, and Jennifer Adamson. Further, he studied at the St. Joseph's School and Professional Children's School. Also, he attended the School of American Ballet, where he learnt ballet.
