Career

Monica Barbaro started the journey towards her career in 2012 by playing a small role in the romantic drama film “Hemingway & Gelhorn”.

She also appeared in short films titled “Touchdown”, “Table for One” and “Tomas and Jackie”.

In 2015, she landed the role of “Brenda ‘Bentley’ Miller” in an episode of a science fiction crime drama television series titled “Stitchers”.

She appeared as “Ella Koha” in action television series titled “Hawaii Five-O”.

She got a breakthrough by appearing in the main role of “Yael” in the 2016 American dream television series titled “Unreal”. She is also known for her portrayal of “Chloe Edwards” in the crime and legal drama television series “Notorious” in 2016. She was also featured as the main character titled “Cora Vasquez” in the streaming comedy-drama television series “The Good Cop”.

She played the role of “Assistant State’s Attorney Anna Valdez” in the action drama television series “Chicago P.D.” from 2016 to 2017, as “Assistant State’s Attorney Anna Valdez” in the 2017 American legal drama television series “Chicago Justice” and as “Nora Cooper” in 2017 comedy-drama action television series “Lethal Weapon”.

She also made her appearance as “Lisa Apple” in the American television sitcom titled “Splitting Up Together” and as “Liz Melero” in the 2019 crime drama television series titled “Stumptown”. Moreover, she will provide her voice for the character “Meagan” in her upcoming television series “Army of the Dead: Lost Vegas”.

She began her filming career by playing the role of “Peggy” in the 2015 American film titled “America Is Still the Place” and as “Phoenix” in the 2022 American action drama film titled “Top Gun: Maverick”. She also performed in the video game titled “Forspoken” in 2022.
