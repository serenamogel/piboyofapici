How old is Kimberly Atkins from MSNBC Today?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, April 17, 2024

How old is Kimberly Atkins from MSNBC Today?- Kimberly Atkins, the Afro-American journalist, author and public speaker is widely known for her work as a senior opinion writer and columnist for Boston Globe Opinion. She is also an MSNBC contributor, co-host of the Politico Podcast #SistersInLaw, and guest host on WBUR’s On Point.

Who is Kimberly Atkins?

On 30th March 1975, Kimberly Atkins was born in Michigan in the United States of America. 

Regarding her education, she went to Wayne State University to complete her education. Apart from this, she has also been a part of other universities like Boston University School of Law, Boston University College of Communication, and the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism. She has completed her graduation, high school, and post-graduation all from well-recognized universities. She also got herself interned at the Boston Globe.

Atkins has kept most information about her personal life including her childhood, parents and siblings totally out of the public eye as nothing is known about them.  

What is Kimberly Atkins’s age, height and weight?

Born on March 30, 1975, Kimberly is 48 years old and an Aries according to her birth sign. 

With her black eyes and black hair, she stands at a height of 5 ft 7 inches and weighs 70 kg.

What is Kimberly Atkins’s net worth?

From her successful career, she’s earned an estimated net worth of approximately $1 million. 

What is Kimberly Atkins’s Nationality and Ethnicity?

According to her nationality, Kimberly is American and belongs to an Afro-American ethnic group. 

What is Kimberly Atkins’s profession?

Talking about her career, she served as the Washington bureau chief at the Boston Herald. There, her work was associated with the live coverage of the White House, Congress, the U.S. Supreme Court, and national news. She also appeared as a political commentator on various national and international news channels and radio shows. Till now, she has worked with many media outlets including NPR, Sky News(U.K.), CNN, NBC News, Fox News, PBS, and CBC News (Canada). Kimberly has also served as the guest post on the morning show “Washington Journal” on C-SPAN. She has interviewed various personalities including lawmaker figures, public policy experts, and journalists.

She has also talked about the various issues on Capitol Hill. Previous to her work with media outlets like CNN, Fox, CBS, NBC, and PBS, Kimberly worked as Washington bureau chief for the Dolan Company newspapers. Dolan Company newspapers are a group of legal and business publications. Therewith the newspaper company, her work was associated with the covering of supreme court highlights. She has covered the reporting of the Boston Globe and Journal News in Westchester County, New York. Before getting into the field of journalism to do jobs, Kimberly was a trial and appellate litigation attorney in Boston. 

Presently, Atkins is an MSNBC contributor whose work is to provide on-air analysis. She also produces comments on the political news of the day. 

Who is Kimberly Atkins married to?

Kimberly is in a union with her beloved and handsome man of life named Greg Stohr. As per the reports, they set the wedding plans and tied the knot in May 2020. 

Does Kimberly Atkins have children?

Presently, Atkins has been blessed with two children whom she had with her husband, Greg.
