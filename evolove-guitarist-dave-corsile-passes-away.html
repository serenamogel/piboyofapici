Evolove Guitarist Dave Corsile Passes Away

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

The music industry is in mourning and shock following the unexpected passing of Dave Corsile, an incredibly talented bass and guitar player. Known for his remarkable musical abilities and his big heart, Corsile died due to a sudden heart attack.

Dave Corsile Musical Journey

Corsile was an integral part of the band Evolove, where he showcased his exceptional guitar skills. His musical journey also included stints with legendary rap-rockers Downset, Supervillain, Hearts on Fire, Dead Verses, and Thrown into Exile. His talent and dedication to his craft left an indelible mark on each band he was a part of, and his impact on the music community is undeniable.

The Man Behind the Music

Beyond his musical accomplishments, Corsile was known for his kind-hearted nature and willingness to help others. He was the type of friend who would drive you to the airport, help you move, or simply grab a bite to eat when you needed company. His genuine care for others extended beyond his circle of friends, making him beloved by many.

Corsile’s passion wasn’t limited to music; he was an avid horror movie fan and hosted backyard drive-in-style movies at his home. He also participated in the creation of several notable horror films, including Silent Night Zombie Night.

A Tribute to Dave Corsile

Corsile was a sober individual, living his life with kindness and positivity. Many lives were positively impacted by his generosity and love of life. Those close to him have shared heartfelt tributes, emphasizing the importance of cherishing friendships and not taking those we care about for granted.

As we mourn the loss of Dave Corsile, we remember a man who was not only an extraordinary musician but also a kind-hearted individual who made a significant impact on those around him. Our thoughts and condolences go out to his family, friends, and fans during this difficult time.
