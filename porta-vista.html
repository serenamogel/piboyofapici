Porta Vista - Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokmon encyclopedia

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 22, 2024

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Porta Vista is an anime-exclusive location in Kanto. It is located on the Island of the Giant Pokémon. It contains two cities, Acapulco (Japanese: アオプルコ Aopulco) and Hutber Port (Japanese: ハトバポート Hatoba Port), which appeared in Beauty and the Beach and Tentacool & Tentacruel, respectively.

The fact that the two different towns were referred to as Porta Vista in the dub is a side-effect of Beauty and the Beach initially being skipped. At the end of the prior episode, the dub showed a wide shot of Acapulco to lead into Hutber Port in the next episode, both of which were called Porta Vista so that Beauty and the Beach could be skipped without breaking continuity. In the dub of Beauty and the Beach, the towns remained combined to avoid any continuity errors between it and the episodes surrounding it.

Acapulco

Acapulco (Japanese: アオプルコ Aopulco) is the town featured in Beauty and the Beach. It was first seen in the very end of Island of the Giant Pokémon. It is based on Acapulco, complete with beaches, sunny skies, and a huge ocean. It is a very popular tourist destination, and it was also the setting for many different adventures in the anime.

Brutella and Moe both own restaurants in Acapulco. Ash and his friends had to help rebuild Moe's dock when they crashed their boat into it. Unfortunately for Moe, Brutella's restaurant was more successful, which threatened to drive his restaurant out of business. With the help of Ash and his friends, Moe was able to bring his business sales back up. In the end, Brutella's restaurant was destroyed by Team Rocket's Gyarados submarine.

Acapulco also held a beauty contest, in which Misty, Gary and his cheerleaders, Jessie, and even James participated in.

The name "Acapulco" comes from the Pokémon.com summary of the episode it was featured in and the two following episodes and makes reference to the real city of Acapulco.

Hutber Port

Hutber Port (Japanese: ハトバポート Hatoba Port) is the town featured in Tentacool & Tentacruel.

Here, Brutella's cousin, Nastina, wanted to build a hotel in the middle of the ocean. However, the Tentacool that lived there were being disturbed by the construction and tried to interfere with the project. Nastina hired Team Rocket to get rid of them, but all they ended up doing was forcing one of the Tentacool to evolve into a Tentacruel and grow it to several hundred feet tall. It began destroying all of Hutber Port until Misty convinced it that not all humans are like Nastina, and that all construction would cease on the hotel.

This is also the location where Misty caught her Horsea.

The name "Hutber Port" comes from the Pokémon.com summary of the episode. It was also used on the Topps Trading Card for the episode.
