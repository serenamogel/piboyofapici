Robert Woods bio, wife, children, parents, siblings, net worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Robert Woods is an American football wide receiver who currently plays for the Los Angeles Rams in the National Football League (NFL). He was born on April 10, 1992, in Carson, California.

Woods attended high school at Junipero Serra High School in Gardena, California, where he played football and ran track. He was a highly recruited player and committed to playing college football at the University of Southern California (USC).

At USC, Woods became one of the most prolific receivers in school history. He finished his college career with 252 receptions for 2,930 yards and 32 touchdowns, earning numerous accolades, including being named a two-time first-team All-Pac-12 selection.

In the 2013 NFL Draft, Woods was selected by the Buffalo Bills in the second round with the 41st overall pick. He played for the Bills for four seasons, where he established himself as a reliable and versatile receiver.

In 2017, Woods signed with the Los Angeles Rams as a free agent. Since joining the Rams, he has become one of the team’s top offensive weapons. In 2018, he recorded a career-high 1,219 receiving yards and six touchdowns, earning him the team’s MVP award.

Off the field, Woods is actively involved in charity work, including supporting the Boys & Girls Club of Venice and the Children’s Hospital Los Angeles.
