Meghan Trainor Net Worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, April 19, 2024

When Meghan Trainor released her debut single “All About That Bass” back in 2014, she became an instant pop sensation. The catchy song about body positivity topped the charts in multiple countries and helped launch Trainor’s career as a successful singer-songwriter. Fast forward to 2021, and Trainor has solidified her place in the music industry with hits like “Lips Are Movin'” and “No.” But just how much is the Grammy-winning artist worth? In this article, we take a closer look at Meghan Trainor’s net worth, career highlights, personal life, and more.

What is Meghan Trainor’s Net Worth and Salary?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Meghan Trainor has an estimated net worth of $8 million. Her main source of income is her career as a singer-songwriter, but Trainor has also earned money through endorsements, tour revenue, and appearances on TV shows like “The Four” and “The Voice UK.”

As for her salary, Trainor reportedly earned $4 million in 2020 alone, largely thanks to her role as a judge on “The Voice UK.” Additionally, she earns money from record sales, streaming royalties, and revenue from merchandise sales on her website.

Early Life and Education

Meghan Trainor was born on December 22, 1993, in Nantucket, Massachusetts. She grew up in a musical family; her father was a musician and her uncle, Burton Toney, was a renowned bassist who played with the likes of Aretha Franklin and The Temptations.

Trainor started writing songs when she was just 11 years old and learned to play the piano and guitar during her teenage years. She attended Nauset Regional High School before enrolling at the Berklee College of Music in Boston.

Career

Trainor began her music career as a songwriter, penning tracks for artists like Rascal Flatts and Sabrina Carpenter. In 2014, she released her debut single “All About That Bass,” which became a viral hit and topped the charts in multiple countries, including the US, UK, and Australia.

Since then, Trainor has released multiple albums and singles, including “Title,” “Thank You,” and “Treat Myself.” She has collaborated with artists like Charlie Puth, John Legend, and Kaskade and won multiple awards, including a Grammy for Best New Artist in 2016.

Personal Life

Meghan Trainor married her longtime boyfriend, actor Daryl Sabara, in 2018. The couple met in 2014 at a house party and started dating two years later. In February 2021, Trainor gave birth to their first child, a son named Riley.

Trainor has been open about her struggles with anxiety and panic attacks, which have affected her both personally and professionally. She has also been vocal about her support for body positivity and female empowerment.

Real Estate

In 2016, Meghan Trainor purchased a $4.9 million mansion in Toluca Lake, California. The 9,800-square-foot home features six bedrooms, eight bathrooms, and a variety of extravagant amenities, including an indoor basketball court and a media room.

Legacy

Meghan Trainor’s music has undoubtedly had an impact on pop culture and the music industry. Her songs about body positivity and self-acceptance have resonated with fans around the world, and her catchy, upbeat tracks have become staples on pop radio.

As for her lasting legacy, only time will tell. However, Trainor’s success as a songwriter and performer has already earned her a place in the pop music canon, and her influence is sure to persist for years to come.
